# Clare Jordan Brand System

## Brand Idea

Quiet authority: precise, warm, practical, and human. The brand should feel like a clear room where complex behavioral health, parity, operations, and AI work can be understood without noise.

## Visual Principles

- Lead with Clare's name, judgment, and language.
- Use white space, black type, thin rules, and disciplined grids.
- Avoid stock healthcare imagery, busy gradients, glossy tech motifs, and anything that looks like a generic AI brand.
- Keep the accent color small. It should guide the eye, not become the identity.
- Treat GitHub examples as polished work samples, not developer cosplay.

## Palette

- Ink: `#161616`
- Warm white: `#ffffff`
- Quiet canvas: `#f8f8f6`
- Soft line: `#dedbd4`
- Muted text: `#68665f`
- Clay accent: `#a75f4b`
- Sage support: `#607168`

## Type

Use system sans-serif typography. Headlines should be large but plain. Body copy should be easy to scan, with short paragraphs and no overdesigned callouts.

## Voice

Professional, specific, and approachable. The tone should show competence through clarity rather than big claims.
