About

I work in the messy middle where law, clinical judgment, benefit design, and operations have to become one coherent system.

My strongest work is translating ambiguity into something other people can use: policy interpretations, documentation standards, training narratives, quality checks, audit logic, implementation plans, and executive-ready explanations.

I bring a clinician's eye for what happens to members and families, a health-plan operator's understanding of scale, and a builder's instinct for making workflows clearer with responsible AI and automation.
